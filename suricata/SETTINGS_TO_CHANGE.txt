In /etc/suricata/suricata.yaml make sure these two settings are correct:

HOME_NET: "[172.16.197.0/24]"      # your lab subnet (the network both machines are on)

rule-files:
  - local.rules                     # so Suricata loads our custom rule

Then copy our rule into Suricata's rules folder:
  sudo cp local.rules /etc/suricata/rules/local.rules

Test the config:
  sudo suricata -T -c /etc/suricata/suricata.yaml -v
